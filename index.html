Download AutoScale

AutoScale is a Rhino plugin for accurate layer-based scaling and annotation workflows.

⬇ Download Installer

Compatible with Rhino 7 and 8 – 30-day free trial included.

Features

  • Scale geometry by defined layers
  • Add dimensions and bounding boxes automatically
  • Label with "SCALED 0.995" and manage grouped output
