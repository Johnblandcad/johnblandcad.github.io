AutoScale is a licensed Rhino tool for accurately scaling geometry by layer, with optional annotations, bounding boxes, and dimensional references.

  • Supports Rhino 7 and Rhino 8 (CPython)
  • Precision scaling by factor (e.g. 0.995)
  • Works with user-specified layers like canvas, seams zips, annotations
  • Outputs clean geometry and annotations on organized layers
  • Includes licensing logic with 30-day free trial
